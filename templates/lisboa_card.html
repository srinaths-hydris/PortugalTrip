📝 How to Get Your Lisboa Card

  • Where to buy: Lisbon Airport arrivals desk ("Ask Me Lisboa", 7 AM–10 PM)
  • When to pick up: Evening of Aug 2 when you drop off Car 1, OR Day 7 arrival
  • What to buy: 72-hour card (€159 total: 2 adults + 1 child)
  • When to activate: First use on Day 7 (starts 72-hour countdown)
  • Valid for: Days 7, 8, 9, 10 (Aug 2–5)
  • Reservation needed: If you decide to enter Belém Tower or Jerónimos, book via MMP portal using card serial number
🇵🇹 Porto Note: Lisboa Card doesn't work in Porto (Days 1-4). Porto transport is separate — use Uber or single tickets (€2/ride). Porto costs (~€150 for activities) are completely separate from this analysis.
