## 💡 Practical Tips

### ☕ Coffee Culture
- **Bica** (Lisbon) or **Cimbalino** (Porto) = espresso
- **Galão** = latte (served in a glass)
- **Meia de leite** = half coffee, half milk (in a cup)

### 🥗 Vegetarian Food Guide
Portugal is meat/fish-heavy, but tascas always have:
- **Caldo verde** — Kale soup (ask "sem chouriço" = without sausage)
- **Sopa de legumes** — Vegetable soup (everywhere!)
- **Queijo** — Portuguese cheese plates
- **Omelete** — Omelets (eggs everywhere!)
- **Salada mista** — Mixed salad
- **Batatas fritas** — Fries
- Pizza/pasta available everywhere
- Say: "Vegetariano, por favor" or "Sem carne, sem peixe" (no meat, no fish)

### 💶 Money Matters
- Cash still common! Many places have €10-20 minimum for cards
- Use ATMs attached to actual banks
- Tipping: 5-10% if service was good (not obligatory)

### 🏖️ August Reality Check
- Algarve beaches = PACKED by 11 AM (start at 8:30!)
- 35-40°C inland, 28-32°C coast — plan indoor/shaded activities 1-3 PM
- Book restaurants in Algarve for August dinners (especially group of 6!)

### 🗣️ Essential Phrases
- "Com licença" = Excuse me
- "Obrigado" (male) / "Obrigada" (female) = Thank you
- "Vegetariano, por favor" = Vegetarian, please
