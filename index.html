💡 Quick Tips

☕ Coffee: Bica/Cimbalino = espresso. Galão = latte. Meia de leite = half-half.

🥕 Veggie: “Vegetariano, por favor” or “Sem carne, sem peixe.” Soups, cheese, omelets, salads everywhere.

💶 Money: Many places have €10-20 card minimum. Carry cash! Tip 5-10% (not obligatory).

🏖 August: Beaches PACKED by 11 AM. 35-40°C inland. Book restaurant tables in Algarve!

🗣 Phrases: “Com licença” = Excuse me. “Obrigado/a” = Thank you.
